The folowing shows different cat breeds and their personality trades

Maine Coon cats come from colder climate areas, which is why they have suchg long furr. Nevertheless they are able to adopt to many new situations, surroundings and temparatues. They are very loving and attention focused cats that need additional grooming by their slave to accommodate their long hair. Maine Coon cats also grow bigger than most house cats.

Sphynx cats, often referred to as naked cats, do not have any visible furr and are easily differentiated from any other cat. Their personality can be described as loving but demanding -they will let you know if there is anything, their servant can do to make their lives better - often a blanket or sweater since without furr, in colder climates they are often cold. Their way of letting everyone know that they have to be the center of attention, could also be described as "outgoing".

Bengal cats are a rare breed of cats who are usually very different from other cat breeds. They are intelligent, very energetic, love needing and affectious - which means they need more of your attention than other breeds. They will demand a great playground at your house to get rid of their excessive energy - which of cause includes you as their personal fool. They are not for inexperienced animal owners.

Orange cats are the devils spawn. They are unpredictable in behaviour at any given time. They will change from calm and cuddly to a furry ball of energy equal to a nuclear device in nano seconds. Therefore they are very entertaining but will take no concideration for any losses. Destroyed furniture and lots of cuts on the slaves body included.

A black cat - often mistakingly labeled as a bad omen - are just very cuddly, love needing, attention seeking and very skiddish cats. These porperties leave the cat often confused about if it should hide or scream for human interaction. Note: black cats are usually less prone to become sick - a big plus since black cats are also very clumsy.

Brittish shorthair cats are calm and balanced cats, their energy level is not as high as with other cats, so their zoomies (A cat usually goes crazy a few times a day - called zoomies) are not as bad. They usually will not engage in harmful interactions since they are calm and balanced. But they will not leave your side.

The siamese cats are similar to the spottet european shorthair cat, very playful and entertaining, but love needing and very vocal, just like the back cats!

A spotted european shorthair cat. They are very curious, smart, playful and will make your home a giant playground with no regards for doors or drawers - or any kind of furniture.

Pure white cats count as the rarest kind of cat and mostly act the opposite of black cats. They are very independed, shy and do not want and need much of your attention. Additionally, they often have gene "defects" like different colored eyes which makes them even more unique.
