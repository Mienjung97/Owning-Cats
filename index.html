The pros and cons of owning cats


Pros

  • Cats are self cleaning.
  • If you do not shower, they will clean you, too.
  • No need for an alarm clock - your cat will be hungry every morning and wake you up accordingly
  • Cats can be very cuddly
  • You will love them unconditionally

Cons

  • A cats tongue is very rough - getting cleaned by them usually hurts.
  • No more sleeping in. Your lovely devil will make sure of that.
  • Once a cat is done cuddling, there is a high chance it will notify you by either scratching, biting or both.
  • Some cats will resort to biting and scratching without being cuddly
  • You will still love them unconditionally

Should you own a cat?


  • Do you want to be owned by a cat?
  • Are you home for at least a few hours every day?
  • Are you ready to clean the litter box daily?
  • Are you allergic towards animals or are you a bleeder?

If you answered the first three questions with yes and the last one twice with "no", you are ready to own a cat!
